Minion

The life of a crewman is hard--swabbing decks, patrolling, shooting aimlessly with little hope of hitting anything. No one will remember you and there's a good chance you'll end up with a very hard slap to the face by a dolphin fin. But in your heart, you'll know that you contributed!

Informer

Our operation depends on the efforts of diligent, sharp-eyed people like you, who report dolphin pirate sightings and pass on valuable intelligence to our task force. The ability to stay underwater for at least thirty minutes at a time is required.

Project Director

Do you have a grand vision, great charisma, and a deep and abiding hate of dolphins? Then you may be a great fit for our Director position! Bend our organization to your will. Make a difference! Note that turnover is high in this position due to mortality: Dolphin pirates are conniving and sneaky, but that only makes the challenge more enticing!
